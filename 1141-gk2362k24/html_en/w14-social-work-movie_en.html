Film Appreciation
The Intouchables

French Cinema Classic — Social Work Across Class Boundaries

Director and Cast

  • Director: Olivier Nakache
  • Cast: François Cluzet, Omar Sy

Plot Summary and Why We Recommend It

  • Plot Summary: Based on a true story. A wealthy white quadriplegic aristocrat hires a young Black man who has just been released from prison as his caregiver. Two people from vastly different backgrounds clash and connect, creating sparks of madness and emotion, and building an incredible friendship.
  • Explores social work, class differences, dignity, and the meaning of life.
