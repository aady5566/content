Common Definitions of Human Consciousness

  • Self-awareness: Knowing who you are, having independent thoughts and existence.
  • Subjective Experience: Feelings about the world, internal experiences.
  • Feelings and Emotions: Joy, anger, sorrow, pleasure, and pain.

Self-awareness

The awareness of oneself, including understanding one's own thoughts, emotions, behaviors, motivations, values, and how these factors influence interactions with oneself and others.

  • Cognitive Aspect: Self-knowledge
  • Affective Aspect: Self-experience
  • Volitional Aspect: Self-regulation, self-control, self-expectation
