Presentation Process: 15 Minutes per Group

(A) 10 Minutes: Formal Presentation

Please strictly adhere to the time limit.

[Reminder] The presentation must include five key structures:

  • • Topic Introduction
  • • Topic Significance
  • • Detailed Introduction (AI-Assisted)
  • • Academic Cases/Literature Support
  • • Core Concepts and Key Questions

(B) 5 Minutes: Q&A Session

Led by the Explorer and Navigator from the "next group".

Questioners should ask in-depth questions about the "core questions" and "literature support" in the presentation.

Q&A Rotation Order

Questioning groups: Please prepare 2-3 in-depth questions.

G1 (Food Poverty) Presents ➔ G2 (Love Scripts) Questions
G2 (Love Scripts) Presents ➔ G3 (Health Foods) Questions
G3 (Health Foods) Presents ➔ G4 (Dating Apps) Questions
G4 (Dating Apps) Presents ➔ G5 (Sleep Quality) Questions
G5 (Sleep Quality) Presents ➔ G1 (Food Poverty) Questions

Grading Criteria

[Instructor Evaluation 75% - Announced Next Class]

1. Content Depth and Academic Rigor (25%)

(Explorer / Navigator)

Key: Are arguments supported by high-quality "academic literature"? Is fact-checking rigorous?

2. AI Skills Application (25%)

(Implementer / Explorer)

Key: Is AI used as a "tool" or a "dependency"? Effective use of AI for research, summarization, or creating high-quality visuals (Canva)?

3. Presentation Structure and Oral Expression (25%)

(Spokesperson)

Key: Is the narrative engaging? Is time management perfect?

[Team Evaluation 25%]

4. Team Collaboration and Q&A Performance (25%)

Key: This score includes "Q&A performance" and is primarily determined by "peer evaluation" results.
